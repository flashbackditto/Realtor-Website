About Us

Amelia is a self-motivated and dedicated worker, homemaker, and mother of three with a passion for Real Estate and a drive for success. Born and raised in Placer County, she is proud to know the back roads and the local comforts, as well as having a personal understanding of the area. Amelia graduated from CSUS in 2011 and slowly began working from home as she started a family. She earned her Real Estate license in 2016 after accepting a position as an Office Transaction Coordinator. After a year of studying and working with the contract, she joined Vista Realty Group and began her journey as a Realtor. She loves helping others find their perfect home in a place that she has been lucky enough to call home also.

Brianna’s attention to detail and personable character are well suited to help you find your perfect home as well as becoming your forever agent. Brianna a third-generation Auburn resident as well as a second-generation Placer High School graduate has been in sales all her life. After High School she continued her education, earning a Bachelor’s degree from California State University of Sacramento in 2012 while working full time. She opened a small jewelry business in 2012 in which she utilized her creative talents as well as her passion for giving. A percentage of her sales have always gone to High Fives Non-Profit organization, as well as multiple donations to community fundraisers.
